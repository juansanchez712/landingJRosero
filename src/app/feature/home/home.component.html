I'm a drafter by profession and a 3d artist by passion and because of this my interests are related to design, architecture and archviz. I have worked for more than 10 years providing customers the opportunity to visualize their projects, where the main objective has always been creating an extraordinary closeness to reality; which as a result facilitates the understanding of the conceptual design, finishes and colours, and most importantly, the making of decisions in this respect.

Distribution companies of recognized worldwide brands have given me the opportunity to participate in the development of interior design and specification of furniture, architectural elements, for corporate spaces, interiors, residential and hospitality projects.

I have practiced different digital means of visualization that includes plans, construction details, animations, product presentations.
